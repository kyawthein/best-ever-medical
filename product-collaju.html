Clinical Protocols

Collaju Skin Booster is designed for professional superficial to mid-dermal application. Precise micro-injection or microneedling techniques ensure optimal delivery of the collagen complex.

Preparation & Mapping

Thoroughly cleanse and disinfect the entire treatment area (face, neck, or décolletage).
Apply a high-quality topical anesthetic cream for 20 to 30 minutes prior to the procedure to ensure optimal patient comfort.
Visually map the face into quadrants to ensure an even and consistent distribution of the product across the entire treatment zone.

Application Methodology

Using a 30G or 32G fine needle, administer small micro-papules (micro-droplets) evenly into the superficial-to-mid dermis.
Alternatively, the solution can be effectively delivered using a professional microneedling device to create uniform micro-channels.
After application, perform a very gentle massage over the treated area to help distribute the viscous gel evenly and minimize any localized swelling.
Apply a soothing, sterile cooling mask immediately post-treatment to calm the skin and reduce the appearance of injection marks.
